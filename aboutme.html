A little bit about me

Raiyaan Shah was born in Los Angeles, California on August 3rd, 2007 at the age of 0. His family consists of two parents, Amina and Ijaz, two siblings, Rahimeen and the other one, and the best cat in the entire world, Hugo. At the present moment he has been working on his first album for a year as of April 8th yet still hasn’t got a clue as to when he will be finished (seriously, not a clue). As for his achievements he had completed the Tour de France at the age of 9 months making him the world’s youngest biker to do so and got a time of 12 minutes and 33 seconds even when he had to travel to the U.S to accept his nobel peace prize and his grammy for best solo female performance in the middle.
